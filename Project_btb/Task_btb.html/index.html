ABOUT US

BTB Healthcare is managing your healthcare so much easier and more affordable.

Instead of traditional care with co-pay, deductible and unexpected medical bills, BTB Healthcare is built on a monthly subscription membership (much like a gym membership). It includes unlimited visits to clinics for primary care, urgent care, virtual care, routine laboratory testing and a comprehensive menu of procedures.

Every aspect of your care is coordinated and teams of experts work together.
Specialists will listen to your needs and evaluate your condition from every angle.
